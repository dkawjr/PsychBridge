# PsychBridge — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-04_

## The short version
**PsychBridge collects nothing about you, stores everything on your own device, and sends nothing over the
internet.**

## What PsychBridge is
PsychBridge is an educational/reference companion app for interventional-psychiatry workflows. This public
build is **de-identified by design**: it does not ask for or store patient names, medical record numbers, or
dates of birth. Records are labeled with study/session codes only.

## Data we collect
**None.** PsychBridge has no accounts, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. It makes no
network connections in normal use.

## Where your data lives
Any session information you enter (de-identified codes, parameters, ratings, notes) is stored **only in your
device's local browser storage (IndexedDB)**. It never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone
else. You can delete it at any time from within the app or your device settings.

## Camera
PsychBridge includes an on-device ASL fingerspelling tool to help deaf or non-speaking patients communicate.
The camera feed is processed **entirely on your device** and is **never recorded, saved, or transmitted.**

## Not a medical device
PsychBridge is an educational prototype. It is not a validated medical device, not an electronic health record,
and not a HIPAA-compliant system. Do not use it to store real protected health information.

## Children's privacy
PsychBridge collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Updates to this policy will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.

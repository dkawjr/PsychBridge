## Full description
PsychBridge is an educational, bedside-style companion for interventional-psychiatry workflows — **ECT,
ketamine/esketamine, TMS, and DBS** — in one place. It helps a learner or clinician think through what each
procedure monitors, capture how a patient is doing across a treatment course, and generate a structured
session summary.

- **One app, four modes** — the monitoring panel adapts to Ketamine / ECT / TMS / DBS, each with the real,
  clinically-relevant variables and safety flags, plus a cited reference sheet.
- **Patient-state & distress capture** — a simple distress slider and tappable "I feel…" cards for moments when
  a patient can't easily speak.
- **Validated rating scales** — includes the freely-reproducible **PHQ-9, GAD-7, and QIDS-SR16** with correct
  scoring and response/remission tracking. (Licensed instruments such as MADRS, CADSS, and C-SSRS are shown as
  locked placeholders in this public build and are not included; they require permission to enable.)
- **On-device ASL "Translate"** — a camera tool that recognizes American Sign Language fingerspelling on-device
  to help deaf or non-speaking patients communicate.
- **Structured note generator** — compiles the session into an editable, copyable summary.
- **Private & de-identified by design** — no accounts, no network, no names/MRNs; everything stays on your
  device.

**PsychBridge is an educational prototype, not a validated medical device, and is not a substitute for
clinical judgment, institutional protocols, or a qualified interpreter.**
